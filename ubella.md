# UBELLA AI Coding Agent Session

## Goal
Build and improve UBELLA’s landing page for early customer validation.

UBELLA is a pre-launch consumer beverage brand creating ube-focused ready-to-drink beverages such as Purple Ube Latte, Purple Matcha Latte, and Purple Cold Brew.

## What I used the AI coding agent for
I used an AI coding assistant to help:
- Structure the landing page
- Write clearer website copy
- Create product sections
- Improve the hero section
- Make the brand feel premium, visual, and easy to understand
- Prepare the site for early customer validation and pre-orders

## Example prompt
“Help me build a clean landing page for UBELLA, a purple ube beverage brand. The page should explain what ube is, why it is visually and functionally interesting, show the first drink products, and make customers excited to try it.”

## What the agent helped produce
The session helped us create:
- A clearer hero message around “Purple is the new green”
- Product cards for Purple Ube Latte, Purple Matcha Latte, and Purple Cold Brew
- A section explaining ube as a naturally purple ingredient
- A stronger brand story inspired by how matcha became a global beverage category
- Website copy that was shorter, more visual, and easier for customers to understand

## Why I am proud of this session
This session helped us move from an idea into something people could actually see and react to.

Because UBELLA is a beverage company, not a software company, the most useful technical work right now is building fast digital surfaces for validation: landing pages, product storytelling, early customer interest, and pre-order readiness.

AI tools helped us move faster, but the product direction, brand thinking, and business decisions were made by the founding team.
